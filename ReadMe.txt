S T I C K E R QwQ  M A N A G E R   v 1 . 0

Welcome! This tool allows you to download, organize, and manage your
Telegram Sticker packs locally on your PC.

[ 1 ] QUICK START

Open this folder.

Double-click "PLAY.bat" (or "Sticker_QwQ_Manager.exe").

The app will launch immediately. No installation required!

[ 2 ] FIRST TIME SETUP (IMPORTANT)

To download stickers, the app needs permission to talk to Telegram.
You need a free "Bot Token". It takes 30 seconds to get one:

Open Telegram and search for "@BotFather".

Send him the message: /newbot

Answer his questions (give your bot a name).

He will send you a long code (API TOKEN) that looks like:
123456789:ABCdefGHIjklMNOpqrsTUVwxyz

Copy that token.

Open this App > Go to Settings (Gear Icon).

Paste the token and click "Save".

Done! You can now download as many packs as you want.

[ 3 ] HOW TO USE

(+) ADD PACKS
Click the big "+" card. Paste a link to any sticker pack
(e.g., https://www.google.com/search?q=https://t.me/addstickers/Animals) and click Add.

(>) SEARCH & TAG
Type in the search bar to find stickers by name.
Click a sticker to see details, rename it, or add custom tags
like "funny", "anime", or "reaction".

(c) COPY TO CLIPBOARD
Click a sticker, select a size (Original, Small, etc.), and click
"Copy Image". You can now paste it into Discord, Slack, or Photoshop.

[ 4 ] TROUBLESHOOTING

(!) "The app closes instantly when I open it."
SOLUTION: Make sure you extracted the ZIP file! Do not run the .exe
from inside the ZIP. Extract the whole folder to your Desktop first.

(!) "Downloads aren't working."
SOLUTION: Go to Settings and check your Bot Token. Make sure you didn't
accidentally paste an extra space at the end.

(!) "Where are my images?"
SOLUTION: Open the "Library" folder located right next to this file.
Each pack has its own folder inside.

[ 5 ] KEY SHORTCUTS

• Double-Click a Pack ..... Open Gallery View
• Left Arrow / Right Arrow ... Change Pages
• Enter ...................... Confirm Search / Add Tag
